The Story

Why NyayIQ Exists

Every morning, tax practitioners across India face the same problem — hundreds of new judgements, circulars, and notifications, spread across TaxGuru, Indian Kanoon, Income Tax websites, and GST portals. Finding what's relevant takes hours. Understanding it takes more.

I spent over a decade practicing tax litigation — drafting appeals, arguing before the GST Appellate Authority, Delhi High Court, and ITAT. During that time, I saw how much time practitioners waste just finding relevant case law, let alone analyzing it.

"The law doesn't lack good judgements. It lacks a good way to find and understand them quickly."

NyayIQ changes that. Every day, our system automatically collects the most important GST, Income Tax, Customs, and ITAT judgements from trusted sources. Each one is analyzed by Claude AI — not just summarized, but distilled into what actually matters for a practitioner: the legal issue, the ruling, and what it means for your clients.

You can also upload any PDF judgement you have — a High Court order, an ITAT decision, even a GST Appellate Authority ruling — and get instant structured analysis in seconds.

NyayIQ is built on one principle: your time as a practitioner is too valuable to spend on research that a machine can do better and faster.
